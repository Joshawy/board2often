About Us

Board2Often

A one-person operation built on a lifelong love of the hobby.

Some people discover board games as adults. For me, it started long before that ‚Äî gathered around the family table with games that had nothing to do with Hasbro or Parker Brothers. My parents had a knack for finding the hidden gems, the ones that didn't fit neatly on mainstream shelves. Looking back, we were playing designer-style games before that even meant anything.

That love never went away. If anything, it grew ‚Äî right alongside the hobby itself.

Board2Often exists because games deserve to be played, not just collected. My goal is simple: get great games into the hands of players across Canada, and build something that gives back to the community that makes this hobby so special.

That means more than just selling games. It means shining a spotlight on community projects, supporting local game designers, sharing what's happening across the tabletop world, and bringing players the kind of 3D printed inserts and accessories that take the experience to the next level.

This is a one-person operation built on a lifelong love of the hobby. And honestly? There's no other way I'd want to do it.

Welcome to Board2Often. Let's play.
